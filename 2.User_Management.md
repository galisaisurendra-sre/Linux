## Basics about user management

1. System accounts   --> Responsible for running background tasks on your system, they dont have home directory.
2. Regular users     --> They have access to thier own files and directories, they cannot perform admin tasks.
3. Superuser         --> The superuser has unrestricted access to the system, can add/remove users, install softwares, can change system configurtion.
